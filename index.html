About Me

Hello! Welcome to my webpage. A little about myself, I grew up in Council Bluffs, Iowa and then attended the University of Iowa in Iowa City. In 2013 with a degree in hand, knowing not what to do with it, I moved to Portland, Oregon. I ran away from the frigid Iowa winter straight into the arms of the dreary Oregon *every season except summer.* It was here that I met my partner, Thomas. Together we fight over who loves our cat, Bobott (pronounced Bob Ott), the most. Let the record show, it is I who loves him the most.

Background

For the past 10 years I have made a living working in the service industry. With the future of the service industry looking questionable in combination with my desire to be challenged, I decided to make a pivot and try my hand at coding. I have always been very pattern-oriented and have enjoyed learning about human languages; coding seemed like a logial next step.
